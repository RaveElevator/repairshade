The Unlocked iPhone 8 comes in two models for US carriers: A1863 and A1905.

1. The A1863 is the GSM+CDMA model and works on pretty much any carrier, including Verizon and Sprint.

2. The A1905 is the GSM-only version, sold as the carrier model for AT&T and T-Mobile, and won’t work fully with CDMA networks.

There are also two 3GB iPhone 8 Plus models: model #A1864 (Sprint, Verizon) and model #A1897 (T-Mobile, AT&T). Each model number supports different technology and will only work with select carrier networks.
